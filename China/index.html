CHINA

China, the People's Republic of China (PRC), is a vast East Asian nation with a 5,000-year history, the world's largest population (over 1.4 billion), and a diverse geography, ruled by the Communist Party and transformed into a global economic powerhouse since economic reforms began in 1978, blending ancient traditions like Confucianism with rapid modernity.
