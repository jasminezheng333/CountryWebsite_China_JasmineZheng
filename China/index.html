CHINA

China, officially the People's Republic of China (PRC), is a vast East Asian nation known for its huge population (over 1.4 billion), ancient continuous civilization (4,000+ years), diverse geography (mountains, plains, deserts), and rapid modernization, operating as a communist state with a market-based economy, with its capital at Beijing and Shanghai as its largest city. It's the world's most populous country (recently surpassed by India), fourth-largest by area, and features a rich history of dynasties, cultural influence, and major global economic power today.
